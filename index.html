Location, Location, Location!

The panoramic views are enjoyed from every room in this immaculate and stylish Villa located on the exclusive, sought-after coastline of Cumbre del Sol. South-facing and in an elevated position, Casa Windlenook was lovingly designed and built by the owners to maximise the fabulous location. Views south are of The Mediterranean (so close you can hear it!), south-west of the rugged conservation area on the hill opposite, and west towards the Sierra Bernia mountains for a dramatic skyline and just fabulous sunsets.

Located on the Costa Blanca half way between Javea and Moraira, Cumbre del Sol is within an area considered by the World Health Organisation to have one of the most environmentally perfect climates in the world. It is a truly extraordinary hillside location with it’s own private beach, Moraig beach, just 5 minutes drive from the villa. On a clear day, magnificent views include east to Ibiza, Calpe’s famous Penon de Ifach and the stunning Montgo or 'Elephant' mountains. Alicante or Valencia airports are easily accessible.

Every imaginable comfort is provided for in this truly luxurious home.
